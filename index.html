What is Elysia MC?
Elysia MC functions as a modded SMP in Minecraft 1.12.2, operating on the modpack Elysia: A World Reshapen. Our objective is to create a server that fosters extensive user interaction, rich lore, as well as elements of geopolitics and warfare.

What mods do you have?
We have a wide variety of mods.
You have the option to construct extensive industrial facilities using Immersive Engineering, explore outer space through Galacticraft, experiment with programming via OpenComputers, and establish a substantial military using Flans' mod.

Is there anything else I need to do?> It is recommended you join the Discord server. It contains important information and announcements. It is also a way to contact the admins and to engage with the rest of the community.
